Basic Crepes

Here is a simple but delicious crepe batter which can be made in minutes

It's made from ingredients that everyone has on hand.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup water
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  1. Step 1

    In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and the eggs. Gradually add

    in the milk and water, stirring to combine. Add the salt and butter; beat until

    smooth.

  2. Step 2

    Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or

    scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each

    crepe. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface

    evenly.

  3. Step 3

    Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen

    with a spatula, turn and cook the other side. Serve hot.
